    I have significant experience in the finance sector, including critical projects like the London Stock Exchange, where reliability, precision, and security are key. I've also worked in e-commerce, ensuring high-quality customer-facing applications, and in software development, focusing on scalable and maintainable solutions across various domains.

  • What tools or technologies do you use for project management and communication?

    I utilize Jira for task and issue tracking and Slack or Microsoft Teams for effective team communication. Confluence is another key tool I use for documenting processes and sharing knowledge across teams.

By collaborating with developers and stakeholders, I proactively identify and resolve potential issues early in the development cycle.

    I stay current by actively participating in QA and development communities, attending webinars, and reading industry-leading blogs and resources.
